Overview

Purpose

To inform people about the NBA Nuggets Basketball team. My website has pictures, highlights, news, and statistics to showcase the multiple facets of the Nuggets team. Used as somewhat of a fanpage, people can come here to read about the latest happenings with the Nuggets. I build from the ground up, so if people don't know what the Nuggets are all about, they can find out from my website!

Audience

My audience ranges everwhere from people who have been nuggets fans for years to those who are just starting to get into NBA basketball and following the Nuggets. To target all audiences my website will have everything from basic news and player names to last years and previous game stats.
